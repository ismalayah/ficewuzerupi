Trivia

This card is one of numerous cards to feature an illustration that is part of a narrative. Following on from the scene on the Charmeleon card, the boy is now a young man, and his Charmeleon has evolved into Charizard. Their tale of friendship continues, with Charizard shielding the young man from snow.

Origin

Combustion Blast is an attack that first appeared on Charizard-EX from Flashfire.
