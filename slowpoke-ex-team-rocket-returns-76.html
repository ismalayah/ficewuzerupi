Slowpoke (Japanese: ヤドン Yadon) is a Psychic-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the EX Team Rocket Returns expansion.

Card text

Dense
どんかん
 
Any damage done to Slowpoke by attacks from your opponent's Evolved Pokémon is reduced by 10 (after applying Weakness and Resistance).
Bite
かみつく		10

Trivia

Origin

Bite is a move in the Pokémon games that Slowpoke cannot learn. Dense's Japanese name is that of Oblivious, an Ability that Slowpoke can have.
