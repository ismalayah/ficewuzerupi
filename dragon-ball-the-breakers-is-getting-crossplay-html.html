Dragon Ball: The Breakers Is Getting Crossplay!

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  351 words  |  Martina Birk

Dragon Ball: The Breakers, a thrilling game launched on October 14, 2022, is about to level up its gameplay. Until now, players on different platforms like PlayStation, Xbox, PC, and Nintendo Switch couldn’t team up. But things are changing! There’s exciting news on the horizon – soon, gamers from all platforms can join forces and play together!

Why Crossplay Matters

Previously, The Breakers kept gamers locked within their platform communities. PlayStation buddies couldn’t team up with their pals on Xbox, PC, or Switch. But with crossplay, these divides will vanish. Friends scattered across different consoles can finally team up for some epic battles!

A Game That Packs a Punch

In this game, players dive into an exciting survival challenge. They’re everyday folks stuck in a time warp, trying to escape using their smarts and gadgets. But here’s the twist – iconic Dragon Ball Z villains like Frieza, Cell, and Kid Buu are the enemies! They’re after the players, ramping up the excitement.

Two Sides of the Coin

In The Breakers, players take on roles – survivors or raiders. Survivors use every trick in the book to outsmart these Dragon Ball baddies. Meanwhile, the raiders focus on hunting down the survivors, making every move count. It’s a thrilling cat-and-mouse game where strategy is key.

Make It Your Own

Customization is huge in The Breakers. Players get to tweak characters, unlock cool stuff, and tailor their gaming experience. This adds a unique touch to every battle, making it more personal and exciting for each player.

The Big Change

The game’s makers surprised everyone by announcing the introduction of crossplay. Before, they had no plans for it despite fans asking for it. But now, they’re breaking barriers, allowing players from different platforms to team up and battle together.

FAQ: What’s Coming?

Will cross-platform play be added soon?
Yes! The Breakers is finally getting crossplay features.
Can PlayStation players join their Xbox friends in games?
Absolutely! PlayStation, Xbox, PC, and Switch players can team up.
How important is customization in the game?
Customization is a big deal, letting players make the game their own.
